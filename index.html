6 Week Fantasy Sports Leagues. Draft a new team each week!

FantasyThrone is a weekly fantasy sports company that allows users to enter 6 week long fantasy sports leagues. Users can enter contests and win money in 4 major American sports (Football, Basketball, Baseball, & Hockey). We combined the best features of full season leagues and daily fantasy to form our 6 week format. Draft new fantasy teams each week and sit atop the throne of your fantasy league!
